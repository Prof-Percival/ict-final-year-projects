What documents are needed to reserve/rent a vehicle ?

Renters must have a valid driver's license and identification
(South African ID or Passport). The minimum age is 18 years old, and the driver's license must have been valid for at least one year. It is a legal requirement in RSA to have your driver's license on you at all times while driving a vehicle.

How old do I need to be to rent a vehicle? 

In South Africa you need to be 21 years or older to rent a car and must have held a valid driver license for a year.

Can International Visitors rent vehicles at CRS Rentals ? 

Yes, International Driver License Requirements:

Foreign driver license will be accepted on condition that: 
The license is in English.
The licence has a photo or ID/passport number on it. 
The license has a signature on it. In the event that a photo is not present, the ID document or passport with a photo is required for the identification of the customer.
The ID/passport number should match the number on the license. 

In the event that the license is in another language, an international driver's license should accompany the foreign driver's license. No driving permits or certified copies/copies of the original driver's license, may be accepted. A translated version of the license, stamped by the embassy, can be accepted when accompanied by the license. 

International visitors must also produce a valid passport. To avoid complications when collecting a vehicle we advise foreign travelers to produce a driver's license which has been issued in the same country as their passport. 

How and When do I pay for the vehicle rented?

When booking on the CRS Rentals Software you have the choice of paying by means of Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) or when collecting your car. By choosing to pay at the time of booking you will receive a discount on your rental. When booking through the CRS Rentals Call Centre or directly with the branch you will only have the option to pay on collection. 

We only accept the following methods of payment:
Credit cards
Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT)

Can I rent a vehicle for longer that a month?

Yes, you can rent a car for a maximum of 365 days with us at affordable prices. You can book your monthly rental on the CRS software, as per the normal booking process.
